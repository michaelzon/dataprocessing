Voter turnout is defined as the ratio between the number of voters to the number of persons with voting rights. The last national election is considered.
Perception of corruption is the percentage of respondents that replied "Yes" to the following question: Is corruption widespread throughout the government in this country, or not?
Share of labour force with at least secondary education is the percentage of adults aged 25 to 64 holding at least an upper secondary degree over the population of the same age. This share is portrayed with degree of coloring the point holds and its radius
All measures are in percentages from 1 to 100%
Source: (OECD)
